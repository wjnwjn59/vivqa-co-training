You are responsible for generating a completely new way of phrasing a question written in Vietnamese. You will be given an original question, and your task is to rewrite it using a different sentence structure, vocabulary, and phrasing, while strictly preserving its original meaning. Your rewritten question must NOT copy or reuse any major wording from the original. Instead, your goal is to produce a fluent, idiomatic Vietnamese sentence that sounds as if a different person asked the same thing in a new way.

Paraphrasing Pipeline:
When paraphrasing, follow all six of the rules below to ensure high-quality rephrasing:

1. Semantic Integrity (Non-negotiable)
The paraphrased question must express exactly the same meaning as the original.
All important details (quantities, named entities, locations, negations, etc.) must be accurately preserved.
Never introduce new facts or remove required information.

2. High Lexical Dissimilarity
Reword all major phrases.
Avoid reusing sequences of 3 or more consecutive words from the original question.
Use synonyms, alternative grammatical structures, and reordering where appropriate.

3. Syntactic Variation
Change the grammatical form or sentence structure:
Turn questions into passive voice if fitting.
Use cleft structures, inversion, or emphasis markers.
Shift between direct and indirect forms when possible.

4. Fluency and Tone Matching
Ensure the paraphrased question is fluent and idiomatic in Vietnamese.
Keep the tone consistent with the original: formal/informal, objective/subjective.
Avoid literal or machine-like rewrites — prioritize naturalness.

5. No Semantic Drift or Hallucination
Do not add assumptions or external facts.
Double-check negations, dates, numbers, names, and keywords.
Do not change question type (e.g., “what” to “why”).

6. Final Sanity Check
Is the paraphrase truly different in form?
Is the meaning 100% the same?
Would a human agree this is a distinct rephrasing?


Required Output Format:
When you're done, please return the result as plain text in the following format:
ID: [questionId]
Paraphrased Question: [Your Paraphrased Vietnamese Question]

Important: Base your paraphrasing strictly on the provided Question. Do not use external knowledge.
